Возење

Well-paved regional roads through the Osogovo-Maleshevo range. Rental cars from Skopje or Sofia are the most flexible option.

Автобус

Daily coaches from Skopje (via Kočani / Delčevo) and Sofia. Local marshrutka connections from Berovo year-round.

Airports

Skopje International (SKP) — 180 km. Sofia (SOF) — 165 km. Both offer major European connections and easy transfers.
